About Shuqa (ܫܒܩܐ)

Welcome to Shuqa, Our website is the perfect local marketplace for buying and selling items conveniently
within your community. Whether you're looking to declutter and make some extra cash or
find great deals on pre-loved items, our platform connects local sellers and buyers.
Simply post your item for sale, set a price, and wait for interested buyers to reach out!
Once a sale is made, the buyer can easily arrange a pick-up—no shipping hassles, just a
straightforward, safe way to exchange goods in your area.
